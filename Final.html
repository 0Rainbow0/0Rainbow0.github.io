How to Make Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Milk, plant milk, or water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
  3. Add the butter and mix
  5. Add the milk, salt, and pepper
  7. Mix until the eggs are well combined
  9. Pour the mixture into a pan and cook
